Privacy Policy

How Campus2Air collects, uses, shares, and protects your information.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: March 4, 2026

Overview

Campus2Air is a carpool matchmaking platform for UC Riverside students coordinating shared rides to and from airports. This Privacy Policy explains exactly what we collect, why we collect it, who can see it, and what rights you have over it.

We are not a consumer app, a data company, or an advertising platform. We operate this service as a student project. We collect only what is necessary to make carpools work.

1. What We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Account Information

  • Your UCR Google account email address (@ucr.edu only), collected via Firebase Authentication (Google Sign-In).
  • Your full name (first name and last initial) and phone number, provided when you join or create a carpool.

Carpool Data

  • Flight code, departure airport, departure date, planned departure time, seat count, and any optional notes you enter.
  • Records of carpools you have joined or created.

In-App Notifications

  • System messages about your carpool (e.g., disbandment notices). These are not marketing emails.

We do not collect location data, payment information, device identifiers, or browsing behavior.

2. Why We Collect It

Every piece of data we collect has a single purpose: operating the carpool matching service. Specifically:

  • Your email authenticates your identity and confirms UCR enrollment.
  • Your name and phone number allow matched carpool members to coordinate with each other.
  • Your carpool listing data powers the matching algorithm and display to other students.

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, profiling, or any purpose beyond running the carpool service.

3. Who Can See Your Information

We limit data visibility based on carpool membership:

  • All authenticated UCR students can see: your first name and last initial, flight code, airport, departure date, and departure time.
  • Only students in your carpool group can see: your phone number.

We do not sell, rent, license, or share your personal information with third-party advertisers, data brokers, or any external parties for commercial purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Campus2Air relies on the following third-party infrastructure providers to function. Each is bound by its own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google): handles authentication (Google Sign-In). Google's Privacy Policy applies to authentication data.
  • Vercel: hosts the application. Vercel's Privacy Policy applies to server-level access logs.
  • TiDB Cloud / MySQL: stores carpool data in a secured database.

We do not control the data practices of these providers beyond the scope of our service agreements with them. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

  • Carpool listings are automatically deleted at 11:59 PM UTC on your listed departure date.
  • Carpool membership records are deleted when the associated carpool expires or is disbanded.
  • You may manually leave a carpool or delete your carpool listing at any time.

Account information (email) may be retained for a reasonable period after your last activity to support notifications, but is never used beyond this service.

6. No Background Checks

Campus2Air does not perform background checks, identity verification beyond UCR email authentication, or screening of any kind on its users. Matching is based solely on flight and schedule data.

By using Campus2Air, you acknowledge that you are meeting other students at your own discretion and risk. We are a coordination tool, not a vetting service.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including HTTPS (encrypted connections), secure database access controls, and server-side session management.

No online service is fully immune to security risks. We recommend using a strong, unique password for your UCR Google account, which is your primary credential for this service.

8. Your Rights (Including California / CCPA)

As a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know: You may request a full description of the personal information we have collected about you and how it has been used.
  • Right to deletion: You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not treat you differently for exercising any of these rights.
  • Right to opt-out: We do not sell personal information. There is nothing to opt out of.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us through the Campus2Air repository or via your UCR student email. We will respond within 45 days as required by California law.

9. Children's Privacy

Campus2Air is intended exclusively for university students aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors under 13. If you have reason to believe a minor has used this service, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of Campus2Air after any changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

We will not make retroactive changes that reduce your rights without clear notice.

11. Contact

Questions, data requests, or concerns about this Privacy Policy can be directed to the Campus2Air project repository or to the maintainers directly via UCR student email.
