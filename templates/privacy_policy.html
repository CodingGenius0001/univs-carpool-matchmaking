Privacy Policy

How Campus2Air collects, uses, and protects your information.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: March 4, 2026

1. Who We Are

Campus2Air is a student-built carpool matchmaking platform designed to help UC Riverside students coordinate shared rides to and from airports. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Campus2Air:

  • Account information: Your UCR Google account email address, provided through Google Sign-In (Firebase Authentication). We only accept @ucr.edu addresses.
  • User profile: Your full name (first name and last initial), email address, and phone number. This information is saved to your profile when you join or create a carpool and is used to coordinate party communication.
  • Carpool listing data: Flight code, departure airport, departure date, planned departure time, seat count, and any optional notes you enter.
  • Party membership: Records of which carpools you have joined or created.
  • Notifications: In-app messages (e.g., party disbandment notices) that you can dismiss.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate the Campus2Air service:

  • Displaying your carpool listing to matched students so they can contact you.
  • Connecting you with other students on the same or similar flights.
  • Enabling party features (join, leave, manage carpool groups).
  • Sending in-app notifications about your parties.
  • Sending email notifications about your carpool party (only if you opted in when joining).

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, or any purpose beyond operating the carpool service.

4. How Your Information Is Shared

Your carpool details (first name, last initial, flight code, airport, date, and departure time) are visible to other authenticated UCR students who are searching for carpools. Your phone number is only shown to students who have joined the same carpool party as you.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

We use the following third-party services to operate Campus2Air:

  • Firebase (Google): Handles Google Sign-In authentication. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • SerpApi: Used to look up real-time flight information. Only flight codes and airport codes are sent — no personal information.
  • Vercel: Hosts the application. Subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Resend: Used to send email notifications when you opt in. Your email address is transmitted to Resend solely to deliver these messages. Subject to Resend's Privacy Policy.
  • TiDB Cloud / MySQL provider: Stores carpool and party data securely.

5. Data Retention

Carpool listings are automatically deleted at 11:59 PM UTC on your listed departure date. Party membership records are deleted when the associated carpool expires or is disbanded. You may also manually leave a party or delete your carpool at any time.

Account information (email) may be retained in our database for a reasonable period after your last activity to support notifications and history, but is never used beyond the scope of this service.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure database storage, and server-side session management. However, no online service is 100% secure. Please use a strong, unique password for your UCR Google account.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. You may also leave carpool parties or allow your listings to expire automatically. Because this service is restricted to UCR students, you can stop using it at any time by simply not logging in again.

8. Children's Privacy

Campus2Air is intended for university students (18+). We do not knowingly collect information from minors under 13. If you believe a minor has used this service, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Campus2Air after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out through the Campus2Air project repository or contact the maintainers directly via your UCR student email.
